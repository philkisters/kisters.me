# Settlementy

Settlementy is a multiplayer idle game built around cooperation. Players share a settlement and must coordinate resource gathering, crafting, and defense to keep it running and growing.

The design focuses on making every player's contribution feel meaningful, even when they are offline, through an idle loop that rewards consistent collaboration.

## What it covers

- Shared resource management across multiple players
- Idle progression that advances while players are offline
- Crafting system for building and upgrading settlement structures
- Collaborative defense mechanics
