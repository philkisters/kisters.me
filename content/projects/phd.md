# PhD — Personal Hope Dashboard

The Personal Hope Dashboard gives PhD students a simple way to track how their hope level changes day to day across the long arc of thesis work.

The idea came from the PhD Simulator and the very real emotional ups and downs that come with academic research. Logging a single value each day makes it easy to spot patterns and reflect on the journey over time.

## What it covers

- Daily hope level input and storage
- Time-series visualization of hope over the thesis timeline
- A lightweight, low-friction interface designed for daily use
- Inspired by the PhD Simulator project
