# SynthAIa

SynthAIa is a card game companion tool for building teams of 15 champions with different strengths in offense, defense, and magic.

The goal of the project is to make each run feel different while keeping the setup and balancing loop fast enough to experiment with new ideas.

## What it covers

- Champion generation and ability assignment
- Team composition tracking
- A companion workflow for testing new card and dice mechanics
